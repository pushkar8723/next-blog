During Covid, when everyone was stuck at home, I started exploring my interest. I realized that I like sharing my projects
with the world. However, it was very hard to get the people to try it out with just a tweet about it. This was also the
time when I fell in love with GraphQL and was trying out everything that used GraphQL. I even created a
[Fullstack Serverless Template](/blog/fullstack-serverless-template) using Apollo. So naturally my search led me to
[Gatsby](https://www.gatsbyjs.com/).

I felt that I found exactly what I needed and so I used [gatsby-starter-blog](https://www.gatsbyjs.com/starters/gatsbyjs/gatsby-starter-blog)
with a lot of customizations to create my [blog](https://github.com/pushkar8723/blog). I got a lot of things correct.
It was simple, React based, used MDX for creating pages, generated RSS feed, optimized images. However, I needed few
more pages to showcase my projects and work experience. When I share links on social media, I wanted the image tile
to also show, like you see below.
